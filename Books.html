Top Books

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

J.K. Rowling’s timeless classic that introduced us to the wizarding world. Follow Harry as he discovers magic, friendship, and courage at Hogwarts — a story that continues to inspire readers of all ages.

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho’s best-selling novel about dreams, destiny, and self-discovery. It’s a poetic journey that reminds you to follow your heart and trust the path of your own story.

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

Mark Manson’s brutally honest take on self-help — showing that happiness doesn’t come from positivity alone, but from caring about the things that truly matter in life.

Atomic Habits

James Clear breaks down the science of habit-building into small, actionable steps that actually stick. It’s a practical guide to becoming just 1% better every day.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Harper Lee’s powerful story about justice, morality, and empathy set in the American South. It’s a moving novel that still resonates deeply with today’s readers.
